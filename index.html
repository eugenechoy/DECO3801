What is Brain Switcha?

BrainSwitcha is a research project dedicated to finding a possible link between a person's Binocular Rivalry Rate and possible mental disorders. Being involved in the project means conducting a 20 minute long test while wearing 3D glasses. The testee is required to watch a cross hatching animation and record each time they notice a switch in their perception.

Binocular Rivalry Rate

Binocular rivalry is the phenomenon of visual perception in which your vision switches between two given images. A binoculary rivalry rate is the rate at which this phenomenon occurs.
